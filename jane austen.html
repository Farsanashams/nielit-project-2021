Jane Austen

Jane Austen is Born Jane Austen is
born in Steventon, England on
December 16, 1775.
She is the seventh of eight children
born to William and Cassandra Austen,
and one of only two daughters.
Throughout her life, her older sister,
also named Cassandra after
her mother, is her closest friend.

First Schooling 1783 Cassandra
and Jane Austen are sent
to Oxford, England to be educated
by a private tutor named Ann Cawley.
Both girls contract typhoid
fever during an outbreak
and return home to Steventon
