Enthralling Woods
Kumaranasan (translated from Malayalam)

Beholding the charms of-
the autumn in the woods,
Running and seeking the-
Tendrils, fruits and florets;

Waving the cherished garbs,
in the breeze, that damsel
She rejoices playfully,
hoping like a butterfly.

Look above in white
crowd the cloud in the sky,
Like the blossom of twigs
of Pala trees in the woods

Far ahead, the white clouds
drift in the winds like
the ashened cranes

What a gracious glimpse !
Oh damsel, like your body,
the gorgeous skyfield of
the forest of the lotuses.

Following this kind in the way,
she embraces the shoulders
of beloved in the quest of silence,
Janaki whispers to him.

Look at the blossom of
White lillies in row like,
the fallen stars from sky
twinkling in the brook
