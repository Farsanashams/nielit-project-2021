Ineffable Dawn

There shall be a cold spring
Beyond deciduous nights
and flaring days.

There shall be a shade
unfurled by a tree of infinite
expanse of benevolence.

There shall be a fist of mud
that sow my eyes
and blossom to epiphany.

There shall be a perpetual stream
of an ineffable dawn
that quench the dezires of the Ages.
