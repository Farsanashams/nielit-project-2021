DISILLUSION

Gayatri

Disillusions are like
there is opium throughout
your body and you got
nothing to do with your sins

Am I in disillusions?
maybe yes
what else to call it
when all the truth
you believe in are
just the shadow
behind it
and you are still
believe that reflections
are truth and
my truth are just shadows

My past was also a shadow
my present too
Now I got to look into a
future which is nothing
but a supershadow.
